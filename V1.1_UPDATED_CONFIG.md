## Updated Bet Sizes

### Previous (faster, simpler)
- **Flop:** 50%, 100% | Raises: 2.5x
- **Turn:** 50%, 125% | Raises: 2.5x
- **River:** 75%, all-in | Raises: 2.5x

### New (more granular, closer to GTO Wizard)
- **Flop:** 50%, 100% | Raises: 3x, 5x
- **Turn:** 50%, 100%, 150% | Raises: 3x, 5x
- **River:** 75%, 150%, all-in | Raises: 3x, 5x

**Impact:**
- More actions per street → larger game tree
- 2 raise sizes instead of 1 → more branching
- Turn has 3 bet sizes instead of 2 → even more nodes
- **Estimated tree size increase:** 2-4x larger
- **Estimated solve time:** 1-4 hours (vs 7-40 min before)

## Why These Changes

1. **3x and 5x raises** - more realistic than single 2.5x
2. **Turn: 50%, 100%, 150%** - better coverage of bet spectrum
3. **River: 75%, 150%, all-in** - more granular sizing for endgame
4. **More similar to commercial solvers** like GTO Wizard
5. **Still practical** - not going crazy with 7+ bet sizes per street

This is a good middle ground between the simple v1.1 and a full commercial solver.
